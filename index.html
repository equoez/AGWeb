Order of the Argent Gryphon

Formally known as the Order of the Argent Gryphon, they are a chivalric order devoted to honour and justice. From their mountain hideout, they watch and defend the lands against corruption and evil, guided by faith, strength, wisdom, and the old oaths of their predecessors.

Argent Codex

The Argent Gryphon adheres to a codex — a set of regulations for daily life, behaviour, and values — emphasising chivalry, piety, and a strict moral code. Written by Roderic Rivershaw, it outlines the ideal conduct for a Gryphon, encompassing both monastic life and military service.

Exhort Righteousness Let thy steel and magic serve justice.
Shield the Innocent Stand between the helpless and harm, though it cost thee all.
Serve with Humility Let pride be cast down, and duty be thy crown.
Seek Wisdom Knowledge is the flame by which corruption is purged.
Show Mercy, Without Weakness Strike not in wrath, nor stay thy hand when retribution calls.
Keep the Vigil Wherever the Gryphon flies, let no infamy go unchallenged.
Die with Honour If thy end must come, let it be noble and unafraid.

Between the Argent Gryphon and the Circle of Stonehaze

Article II — Alliance in Arms: Mutual defence against evil.
Article III — Mutual Aid: Assistance when needed.
Article IV — Exchange of Knowledge: Shared intelligence.
Article V — Hospitality: Open guildhalls to one another.
Article VI — Fellowship: Regular meetings between members.

Silverhand Decree

Let it be known throughout Vandura that Governor Percy Silverhand, by royal right, hereby grants full sovereignty to the chivalric Order of the Argent Gryphon.

The Silverhands of Liberty Bay reaffirm the old oath, empowering the Warden's Council with the charge of vigilance in the Vandura Mountains. They are free to anoint warriors of the true Gods, and to arm each with the Griffin Shield - a symbol of strength and courage.

May the Argent Gryphon bring order to chaos, whatever the cost may be.
